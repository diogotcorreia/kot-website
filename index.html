About Us

The Royal Orienteering Technologists (KOT) is an orienteering club and THS Student Union Association at KTH, dedicated to orientation and running. We organize regular orientation and ongoing training.

The club participates in a number of competitions every year, such as the Nordic Student Championships (NSM) and Pampas Night Nattbudkavle. The association was formed in 1982, and today consists of elite trainers, exercise orienteers and former orienteers. Old athletes, multi-athletes, exchange students and ordinary people also attend the training sessions. Most are, or have been, students at KTH, but representatives from other schools are also, and of course, welcome.
